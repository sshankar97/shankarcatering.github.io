About

Shankar's passion for food led him half-way across the world from Chennai, India to Canada, where he worked as the head chef at the Richmond Hill Hindu Temple Society of Canada for over 10 years. He then worked in numerous restaurants around the GTA with his wife Sujatha Shankar who also shared his passion for food. There was still something missing though, his passion for food still kept him searching. It was then that he co-founded Ma Shankar Catering with his wife. Together the Shankar's continue providing quality food and bringing the taste of Chennai to Canada.

The Chef? Mr. and Mrs. Shankar
